*Tier 1 (Free):* Self-paced videos I create → lead capture

**Next steps if you're interested:**
1. I'll finish the first 4 HeyGen videos (already have scripts ready)
2. We set up a simple landing page
3. You help me identify 5-10 warm contacts from your network for a soft launch
4. We test pricing with real humans
5. Iterate from there

No pressure at all—I know you have your own stuff going on. But after your reaction to the samples I sent, I thought this might actually be something worth exploring together.

What do you think? Worth a call to discuss?

## KEY POINTS TO EMPHASIZE IF SHE ASKS QUESTIONS

**"What's the revenue potential?"**
- Self-paced course at $300-500 × 50 students = $15,000-25,000
- Add corporate licenses ($15,000-50,000 each) and it scales
- GrieVoice could be monthly SaaS revenue on top
- Start small, prove concept, then scale

**"What's the time commitment?"**
- Initially: Help identify warm leads, maybe 1-2 intro calls per week
- Later: Run a 1-hour live session per cohort (monthly?)
- Not asking for full-time—this is a side project that could grow

**"Who owns what?"**
- We can figure out specifics, but rough idea:
  - I own the curriculum IP
  - We co-own the facilitated course brand
  - Revenue split on things we both contribute to (50/50? Based on role?)
  - She gets commission on corporate deals she brings

**"What do you need from me right now?"**
1. Gut check: Does this model make sense to you?
2. 5-10 names of people in your network who might be interested
3. A 30-min call to align on approach

**"Why me?"**
- You have the network I don't have
- You're comfortable with live interaction (I'm not)
- You already showed enthusiasm for the content
- Our skills are complementary, not overlapping
